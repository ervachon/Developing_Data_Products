Documentation:
This study use portugues data students of two schools to predict their final grade.

you have on the left two tab :
  • common : the possibility to choose the course data (portugues course or math course) and the colors of the histograms. You can also go to the data source or download them directly.
  • features selection: you can choose the features that you want to predit the grade. Be carefull don't forget to click the button to update ther formula of the prediction !

    On the main screen you have four tabs :

  • Doumentation (the tab you read),
  • Source and data informations (from UC Irvine Machine Learning Repository),
  • Histograms of the age of students by sex and schools ('GP' - Gabriel Pereira or 'MS' - Mousinho da Silveira),
  • Prediction of the final grade of the students in the selected course,
  • And you can browse the data.
