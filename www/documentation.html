This study use portugues data students of two schools to predict student performance in secondary education (high school).

you have on the left two tab :

  • common : the possibility to choose the course data (portugues course or math course) and the colors of the histograms. You can also go to the data source or download them directly.
  • features selection: you can choose the features that you want to predit the grade. Be carefull don't forget to click the button to update ther formula of the prediction !

    On the main screen you have six tabs :

  • Documentation: Doumentation (the tab you read),
  • Histograms: Histograms of the age of students by sex and schools ('GP' - Gabriel Pereira or 'MS' - Mousinho da Silveira),
  • Prediction: Prediction (wait a few seconds) of the final grade of the students in the selected course,
  • Browse data: And you can browse the data.
  • Source and data information: Source (Paulo Cortez, University of Minho) and data informations (UC Irvine Machine Learning Repository),
  • Map of the university: Localisation (GoogleVis, wait a few seconds) of the University of Minho, Guimar√£es, Portugal,
